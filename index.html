The usually deep purple roots of beetroot are eaten either grilled, bioled, or roasted as a cooked vegetable, cold as a salad after cooking and adding oil and vinegar, or raw and shredded, either alone or combined, with any salad vegetable.

In fact, Jim Halpert once said this about beets, "Fact, bears eat beets. Bears, beets, Battlestar Galactica."
